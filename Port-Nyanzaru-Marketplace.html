Every fare includes what goes over the side at the mouth of the bay, made in one offering, all together; no master itemizes it and none will discuss it. What goes over depends on what's aboard: masters look passengers over on the quay, and heavy magic reprices a berth on the spot, or closes it. Cheaper ways off the island are said to sail from Refuge Bay, and what waits past the Bay instead of the deep water is a matter of some discussion among men who fly no flags.
